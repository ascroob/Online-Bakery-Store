Implenting DMCA Notice & Takedown Policy

Any user of this site may file a takedown request, infringement notice, or dispute claim. This claim is filed under the /DMCA section of The Bakery's site. All claims submitted by users are posted on this page, under the section "DMCA Notices". Following are the steps which should be taken by a manager when there are unresolved claims present:

  • Takedown requests: If the takedown request is reasonable and requests for a comment or product to be taken down from the site for reasons such as profanity or irrelevance, please remove the comment/product.
  • Infringement notices: Look into the user's infringment claim. If we are in fact infringing upon their claimed copyright, remove the comment/product.
  • Dispute claim: If there is a dispute claim regarding a takedown request or infringement notice, contact the user for more information with their provided email. Restore any comment/product if the dispute is proved.
  • After any of these claims, contact the user with the provided email explaining which comment/product was removed or restored.
  • When a claim has been handled, mark as "resolved" using the resolved button below the request.
  • To send a takedown notice, email the user at the email provided. In the "DMCA Takedown Notice" section below, mark the notice as sent. A claim is not fully resolved until this email is sent.
  • To update the DMCA Policy statement, click the update button on the /DMCA page and submit the updated statement. It will automatically be posted.
